Installing Wires on macOS

Open the downloaded .dmg file and place Wires in Applications. Wait for the copy to finish, then open the app from that folder.

ℹ️ Blocked on first launch? In Applications, right-click Wires and choose Open. Confirm Open again in the dialog.

Installing Wires on Windows

Double-click the downloaded .exe, then follow the steps in the Wires setup wizard.

ℹ️ Windows SmartScreen may appear the first time. Choose More info to reveal Run anyway, then confirm.
