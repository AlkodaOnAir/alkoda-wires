Workflow

How Wires turns a setup into a readable plan.

In three steps, Wires transforms a hard-to-read installation into a clear, animated and shareable plan.

01

Add your devices

Import a real image, crop it, and place ports exactly where they are on the equipment.

02

Connect the ports

Create cables, choose their type and color, then organize the plan with zones, labels and categories.

03

Show the signal

Group cables into a route, launch the animation, then export interactive HTML or PDF documentation.
